Blogs

Kadhali Price Difference.

The FarmDay mobile app, created for farmers, is already proving successful by helping farmers know the correct selling prices of their crops. Shortly after launch, it revealed significant price variations for *Kathali* bananas within Coimbatore District—selling at ₹40/kg in Annur Taluk and ₹30/kg in Kinathukadavu Taluk on December 4th. This price gap highlights the need for accurate pricing information, which FarmDay provides. Farmers can use the app to determine fair prices, sell accordingly, and share prices with other farmers to ensure better market transparency and fair pricing for everyone in the farming community.

Annur Red Banana Price Variance.

The FarmDay mobile app, tailored for farmers, helps them understand fair selling prices for their crops. After just a few months, it’s already exposing significant price variations. For example, on June 9, 2021, a farmer in Annur Taluk, Coimbatore, sold Red Bananas at ₹27/kg, while another in the same area, unaware of the market rate, sold at ₹18/kg. Even with quality differences, a ₹9/kg disparity is considerable. By using FarmDay, farmers can access accurate pricing, set fair rates, and share prices with others, creating transparency and helping ensure they receive fair compensation for their produce across regions.

Udumalpet Nendhiran Banana Price Difference.

The FarmDay mobile app, created for farmers, helps them understand accurate crop prices. Just months after its launch, it’s already revealing significant price differences across regions. For instance, on June 17, 2021, a farmer in Udumalpet, Tiruppur, discovered that *Nendran* bananas were sold at ₹30/kg in nearby Sathyamangalam on June 16, while he sold his for only ₹21/kg. Sathy Mondhan Banana Price Difference.

A FarmDay user from Sathyamangalam, Erode District, recently shared insights about the app. On July 24, 2021, he sold a bunch of *Monthan* bananas for ₹260, while another farmer just half a kilometer away sold similar quality bunches for ₹160. This illustrates the pricing discrepancies that FarmDay aims to highlight, helping farmers secure fair prices. The app allows farmers to check and share crop prices, providing valuable insights into regional pricing and enabling them to sell their produce at appropriate rates. By doing so, FarmDay fosters transparency and fairness in the agricultural market. Thank you!

Farmday Rasthali Correct Price.

A farmer recently used the FarmDay mobile app to determine the selling price of his *Rasthali* bananas and successfully sold them at the right price. A trader initially offered him ₹180 per bunch, while the market rate in Sathyamangalam was ₹26 per kilogram. Using FarmDay, the farmer realized the market value and refused the lower offer, later selling the same bananas to another trader for ₹300 per bunch. Without FarmDay’s insights, he would have sold them for much less. The app’s primary aim is to help farmers know accurate selling prices, and we are pleased to see its positive impact on many farmers. Thank you!
